For example A cocktail that never seems to go out of style is the Long Island Iced Tea
The potent concoction of rum, tequila, vodka, gin and triple sec finished with a mixture of sweet and sour cola and lemon juice.
Tea isn't actually an addition to the drink, but the ingredients used predominantly the cola, provide the profile of tea with much more flavour.

Ingredients;

1/2 oz vodka
1/2 oz gin
1/2 oz light rum
1/4 oz tequila
1/2 oz triple sec
1 Tbsp. fresh lemon juice or possibly to taste
6 oz cola or potentially to fill broken ice
