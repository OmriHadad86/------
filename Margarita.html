Margarita

Margarita is the The simple mixture of tequila, triple sec and lime juice is often blended with ice but is traditionally served on the rocks.
The cocktail is generally presented in a salt rimmed glass.
Various fruity versions have been adapted from the traditional lime, such as raspberry, peach and strawberry.

Ingredients:

1 (6 ounces) can frozen limeade concentrate
6 fluid ounces Tequila
1 oz Cointreau orange liqueur
2 fluid ounces triple sec
