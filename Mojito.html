Mojito

Originating in Cuba, this refreshing rum-based sip is filled with mint and limeâ€”a perfect combination for sipping by the pool or beach.
If you're craving a little literary cred,
the mojito was also said to be a favorite of author Ernest Hemingway.

Ingredients:

3 mint leaves
2 oz white Rum
0.75 oz lime juice
0.5 oz simple syrup

Muddle mint into a shaker tin, then add ice and all other ingredients.
Shake to chill and strain into a highball glass with ice. Top with club soda if desired and garnish with mint.
